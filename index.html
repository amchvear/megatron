What is a Light Tracking Robot?

A light tracking robot is designed to follow a light source, using sensors to detect light intensity.

How It Works

  • Light Sensors: Detect the intensity of light.
  • Microcontroller: Processes sensor data and makes decisions.
  • Motors: Control the robot's movement.

Components

The basic components needed for building a light tracking robot include:

  1. Light sensors (LDRs)
  2. Microcontroller (like Arduino)
  3. Chassis and wheels
  4. Motors and motor driver
  5. Power supply (batteries)
